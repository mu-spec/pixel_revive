Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

Welcome to PixelRevive ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and photographic data is treated with the highest security and transparency. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, processed, and secured when you use our mobile application, PixelRevive.

🛡️ Zero Data Retention Policy: Your photos belong to you. We do not store, catalog, or permanently keep any of your uploaded or processed photos on our servers.

1. Photographic Data & Image Processing

PixelRevive is designed as a hybrid on-device and cloud-based AI Photo Enhancer. We handle your photos in two secure ways depending on your selected processing mode:

  • Local On-Device Mode: When you process images offline or with Cloud AI disabled, all mathematical filters, skin smooths, sharpness corrections, and crops occur 100% locally on your smartphone's CPU/GPU. Your photos never leave your device.
  • Secure Cloud AI Mode: When you enable Cloud AI (Replicate Integration), the selected image is temporarily converted into an encrypted Base64 stream and sent over a secure SSL connection to Replicate's cloud GPU servers for processing. The server processes the enhancement and returns the image immediately. Your image is immediately erased from the GPU's volatile memory after processing and is never permanently stored on any server.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a smooth, professional user experience, the app collects minimal technical data:

  • Local Settings (SharedPreferences): We store your preferred language selection, premium purchase status, and daily free-saves counts locally on your device to keep the app working offline.
  • Device Permissions: We request permissions to access your photo gallery (to import and save images) and camera (to capture new photos). These are only used when you explicitly trigger these actions.
  • Technical Analytics: We may use basic, non-personally identifiable crash logs to help debug crashes and improve stability.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party service providers to power specific cloud AI and monetization workflows:

  • Replicate API: Runs state-of-the-art AI restoration models (GFPGAN, Real-ESRGAN, CodeFormer). Requests are secure and subject to Replicate's privacy compliance.
  • Google Play Store Billing: Handles safe and secure premium upgrade payments and weekly/yearly subscriptions. We never see or store your credit card details.
  • Google AdMob (Free Tier): Displays small banner or interstitial ads to monetize free users. AdMob may collect device advertising IDs to deliver relevant ads.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/SSL) to secure all data in transit between your phone and our cloud API partners. Because your photos are processed temporarily and immediately deleted, there is no threat of your images being leaked, hacked, or stolen from our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

PixelRevive does not knowingly collect or request personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently processed data of a minor, please contact us immediately, and we will purge any relevant technical logs.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy inside the app's settings section.
