Privacy Policy

PixelRevive - AI Photo Restore & Enhance

Effective date: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PixelRevive collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using PixelRevive, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. App Overview

PixelRevive is a photo enhancement and restoration app. It allows users to improve, restore, upscale, denoise, unblur, colorize, and edit photos using on-device processing and, when selected, cloud-based AI processing.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may process the following types of information:

  • Photos selected by you: Photos are used to provide editing, enhancement, restoration, or AI processing features.
  • Device and app information: Such as device type, operating system, app version, crash/performance information, and general usage information.
  • Advertising information: Such as Advertising ID, ad interactions, approximate location, and device information used by Google AdMob.
  • Purchase information: If you buy premium features, subscriptions, or lifetime access, purchase processing is handled by Google Play Billing.

3. Photos and Image Processing

PixelRevive allows you to select photos from your device. Photos are used only to provide the feature you choose.

On-device processing

Some features may be processed locally on your device. In this case, the photo does not need to be uploaded to our backend for processing.

Cloud AI processing

If you choose Cloud AI mode or a feature that requires cloud AI, your selected image may be uploaded securely to our backend service and/or third-party AI processing providers only to generate the enhanced result. Cloud processing may be used for features such as photo restoration, upscaling, face enhancement, colorization, denoise, unblur, or similar AI tools.

We do not sell your photos. We do not use your photos to identify you. We do not intentionally share your photos except as needed to provide the selected cloud AI feature, comply with law, or protect the app from abuse.

4. Third-Party Services

PixelRevive may use third-party services to operate, monetize, and improve the app. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google AdMob: Used to show banner and interstitial advertisements.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process subscriptions, premium upgrades, and lifetime purchases.
  • Cloud hosting/backend providers: Used to run backend services for Cloud AI features.
  • AI processing providers: Such as Replicate, Fal.ai, or similar providers may be used to process images when Cloud AI is selected.
